Why Cybersecurity?

As cyber threats continue to evolve, organizations require skilled professionals to protect their digital assets, networks, and data from unauthorized access and breaches.

Key Technologies

  • Security Standards: OWASP Top 10, NIST Framework
  • Security Tools: Wireshark, Metasploit, Burp Suite
  • Encryption: AES, RSA, SSL/TLS
  • Threat Detection: IDS/IPS, SIEM (Splunk, QRadar)
  • Penetration Testing: Kali Linux, Nessus

Career Opportunities

Cybersecurity experts are critical for preventing cyber-attacks, data breaches, and ensuring secure system architecture, making this one of the most in-demand career fields today.
